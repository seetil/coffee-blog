A blog: written, coded and imagined by Seetil

Hi! My name is Seetil. I am a restaurant manager/ web developer in training/ tobacco and caffeine addled Partick resident! Writing and coding this blog as the latest in a long string of lockdown hobbies and projects. Anything to keep the furlough delirium at bay.

Few notes about my coffee habits: I order my coffee as whole beans as I prefer to grind them myself. The smell of freshly ground coffee is one of lifes purist pleasures and quite possibly what has driven me to this aparent coffee bean snobbery! I use a french press as opposed to filter coffee - yes there is a difference! The grinds are less fine for a french press and I feel that the coffee infuses much better in a french press giving a fuller taste to the resulting coffee.
