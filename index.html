"Living my best coffee life"

Living in Glasgows west end means great coffee can be found on every other block. Glasgow has no shortage of coffee shops!! But in a bid to save some money I have signed up for a monthly subscription to Pact coffee. Pact is a coffee bean company that sends you a different, high quality bag of coffee beans (whole or ground) as often as you choose and at a very decent price. This blog will document the different beans I recieve while living my best coffee life.

"You never forget your first..."

21/11/20 My first pact delivery has arrived! I recieved a bag of beans called Planalto. It originates from Minas Gerais, Brazil and was grown by Sergio and Mariza Mantovaninni. The package states tasting notes of 'malt milk chocolate shake' and mouthfeel is 'round'. Giving the beans a quick blend in the grinder causes a disgruntled, sleepy flatmate to emerge from her room and a delicious smell of fresh coffee wafting across our little kitchen. After 5 minutes stewing in the french press I poured my first pact coffee. The heavy flavour comes through from the first sip. It tastes almost sweet and has a fantastic fullness to it. Very happy with my first coffee bean delivery. 9/10 would recommend.
